What is Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial Intelligence involves creating machines that can think like humans and imitate their actions. It uses various technologies to enable computers to do things that normally need human intelligence.

How AI Works:

  • AI learns and becomes more intelligent through practice and data
  • Similar to how humans learn - gets better through experience
  • Uses algorithms to learn from experiences rather than being explicitly programmed
  • The more data AI systems have, the better they become at their tasks

AI in Everyday Life:

AI is already integrated into our daily routines through search engines, streaming recommendations, voice assistants, and personalized content delivery systems.
